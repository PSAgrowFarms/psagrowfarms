About Us

A Farm Built on Quality & Integrity

PS AgrowFarms is a dedicated turkey production enterprise headquartered in Kuantan, Pahang. We specialise in raising premium free-range turkeys under ethical, sustainable farming conditions.

Every turkey on our farm roams freely in open paddocks with access to natural sunlight and clean air. Our birds are nourished exclusively with certified organic feed — free from synthetic hormones, antibiotics, and artificial additives.

We supply live and processed turkeys to valued customers across Pahang and the Klang Valley, serving households, restaurants, caterers, and institutions with the same commitment to quality.

"Raised with care. Delivered with pride."

Our Products

What We Offer

Two product types to suit every requirement — from farm operators to home cooks and professional kitchens.

🦃 Live Turkey

Healthy, active birds dispatched directly from our farm

  • Ideal for personal rearing, smallholder farms, or processing centres
  • Birds are inspected by our team before dispatch to ensure they are healthy
  • Carefully selected to meet your size and weight specifications
  • Transported with care to minimise stress and ensure welfare
  • Available for farm-gate pick-up or arranged delivery

🍽️ Processed Turkey

Professionally prepared to food-grade hygiene standards

  • Whole birds available in multiple weight categories
  • Selected cuts available for bulk or institutional orders
  • Hygienically packaged to preserve freshness and quality
  • Chilled or frozen delivery options upon request
  • Ideal for restaurants, hotels, catering companies, and households
  • Consistent sizing for portion-controlled catering operations
